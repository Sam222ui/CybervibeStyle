Cybervibe

In science fiction circles, "cyberpunk" is a genre that often features countercultural antiheroes trapped in a dehumanizing high-tech future, Cyberpunk as a genre is popular because it tends to tell cautionary tales of were we can end up if we aren't cautious. It explore the base natures of humanity from greed to lust, as well as the loss of humanity we can experience from all the new technology. Deep sadness and melancholy aren't common emotions for games to instill within players, but when they do, the hurt tends to linger. For me, Cyberpunk 2077 is exactly that kind of game, and I doubt I will ever shake the bitterness of its endings out of my head.
